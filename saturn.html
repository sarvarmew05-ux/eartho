About Earth

Saturn is a gas giant, the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest planet in our solar system, famous for its prominent ring system. It is primarily composed of hydrogen and helium, is the least dense planet, and has a large family of moons, including Titan. Its name comes from the Roman god of agriculture.
